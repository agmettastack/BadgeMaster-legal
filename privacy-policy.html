Privacy Policy

Product Badge Manager
Effective date: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Product Badge Manager collects, uses, and protects information when merchants install and use the app on Shopify.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Product Badge Manager, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Shop information, such as your Shopify store domain
  • App configuration data, including badge settings, rules, and preferences
  • Product and collection data needed to apply and display badges
  • Limited Shopify account and session information required for authentication and app functionality
  • Technical information such as app logs and basic usage data needed to maintain and improve the app

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Save badge settings and assignments
  • Generate and update storefront badge data
  • Support automatic badges such as Sale, Sold Out, and New
  • Maintain app security, performance, and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

3. Shopify Data

Product Badge Manager accesses Shopify store data only as needed to provide app features. This may include products, collections, metafields, and related storefront display data. We do not sell merchant data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information or store data. We may share information only when necessary to operate the app, comply with the law, enforce our terms, or protect our rights and users.

5. Data Retention

We retain app-related data for as long as needed to provide the app and support merchants. If you uninstall the app, we may delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, unless we are required to keep certain information for legal, security, or legitimate business purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

Product Badge Manager may rely on third-party infrastructure and services to host and operate the app. Those providers may process data on our behalf only as needed to support app functionality.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information. To make a request, contact us using the email below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Product Badge Manager is intended for business use by Shopify merchants and is not directed to children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
